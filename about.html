About Us

At Pop’s Kitchen, we believe food is more than just a meal—it’s a movement. Pop’s Kitchen is a community-powered initiative by the Centre for Social Innovations (Charity Reg. No. C0004715497) with a simple yet powerful idea:

“Every meal you order helps feed someone who needs it.”

We combine quality, home-style food delivery with a deep-rooted commitment to social impact—tackling hunger, creating dignified employment, and uplifting communities through food.

What we do?

Meal-for-Meal Program

Every time you order from Pop’s Kitchen, we donate a nutritious meal to someone in need—elderly individuals, low-income families, and those struggling with food insecurity.

Chef Training Program

We train young individuals, home cooks, and women from underserved backgrounds to become professional chefs—with hands-on training, certifications, and career placement.

Student & Elderly Meal Support

Through our subsidized Tiffin Aid programs, we provide affordable, wholesome meals to college students and senior citizens across the city.
